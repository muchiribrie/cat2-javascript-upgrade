ABOUT US
Born From Passion

Whisk’d Atelier is where dessert gets its glow-up a luxury pâtisserie in Westlands,Nairobi crafting more than just cakes, but full-on edible experiences. Every creation is handmade with precision, artistry, and a little bit of magic, turning celebrations into moments people actually remember.
From elegant wedding cakes to bold custom celebration designs and delicate artisan pastries, Whisk’d Atelier blends classic French technique with modern, aesthetic-driven flair. It’s soft,luxiourious and a little extra but in the best way.

Founder and Owner

What began as a passion for baking and bringing people together has grown into a brand dedicated to creating unforgettable moments through handcrafted desserts. Every cake, pastry, and sweet creation is made with care, creativity, and attention to detail.

At Whisk'd Atelier, we believe desserts are more than just treats — they're part of life's celebrations, milestones, and memories. Our mission is to transform every occasion into something extraordinary, one creation at a time.
