I spoke to the owner of Whisk'd Atelier. She told me that it is a luxurious home bakery that specializes in custom cakes, pastries and desert tables.
The target audience is clients who are looking for luxury and premium desserts for all kinds of events 
from birthdays to weddings to corporate events and even mere special celebrations.
The owner preferred a sophisticated and elegant look with cream, champagne gold, and shades of brown as the main colors.
She was going for something like Dubai luxury(that was the inspiration).
The website is to showcase signature creations, customer testimonials, ordering information and contact details.
The main goal of this website is to help potential customers learn about the brand and place custom orders.
